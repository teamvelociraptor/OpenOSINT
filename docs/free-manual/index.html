THE PROBLEM

Most people use AI for OSINT backwards. They open a chat model, type “find everything on this email,” and trust whatever fluent, confident answer comes back — no sources, no verification, a worrying amount of it invented.

The problem isn’t the model. It’s the missing process.

SAMPLE PROMPT

One of the two core prompts, verbatim — the scoping phase, straight from the PDF:

You are an OSINT analyst in the SCOPING phase. Do not collect
or guess any data yet.

Engagement: [one line on what you are authorized to investigate]
Objective:  [the specific question you need answered]

Output:
1. The objective restated in one sentence.
2. In-scope vs out-of-scope (sources, identifiers, targets).
3. The 3-6 concrete questions that, if answered, satisfy it.
4. The public source types relevant to each question.
5. Legal/ethical flags I should confirm before proceeding.
Ask me anything ambiguous instead of assuming.
